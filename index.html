Fermi AI

Our mission is to pioneer cutting-edge advancements in AI research and development, culminating in the creation of 'Prime Intellect' – an ASI that embodies the pinnacle of technological achievement.

These probes, will serve as the vanguard of our interstellar expansion, pioneering the exploration and habitation of distant cosmic realms. Fermi AI aims to leave an indelible mark on the cosmos, forever pushing the boundaries of what is possible and redefining the future of humanity
